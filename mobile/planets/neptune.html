Neptune

This gas giant is the most dense one of them all and is believed to have formed much closer to the Sun in early solar system history before migrating to its current position. The Great Dark Spot can be found here, which consists of anticyclonic storms with a tremendous speed of 2400 km/h.

Table of Facts

Mass 1.024 x 1026 kg
Orbit Period 164.8 years (Sun)
Radius 24622 km (0.035 *R☉)
Gravity 11.15 m/s2
Temperature 72.15 *K (troposphere)
Moon(s) Triton, Nereid, Despina, Thalassa & 10 others.
